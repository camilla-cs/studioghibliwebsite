🥨 Ghibli-themed Recipes 🥓

One of the most memorable and central part of every Miyazaki's movie are food scenes.
The level of detail and dedication put into crafting those scene are not only enjoyable, they also feel like a sort of calming ritual for both the character and the viewer, so this page is dedicated to all the food-lovers that would enjoy to recreate the recipes from their favorite Ghibli movie!
Here are some videos and blogs on how to try the delicious food by yourself:

- Herring pot pie from Kiki’s Delivery Service 🐟

Video by Choki

Kiki’s Delivery Service is my favorite Ghibli movie. I watch it whenever I need comfort. Me and Kiki share similar experiences since we both moved far away from our families, alone and having to rely on our own strenght.
The herring pie scene is pivotal in Kiki's disillusionment towards people, and she comes to understand that not all people have good intentions.
From this lesson though she will earn a wonderful friendship with 'Madame', and she will have the chance to meet people who will become their friends, the artist and Tonbo in particular.
