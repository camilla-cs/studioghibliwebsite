Here's a list of animes and movies with similar ghibli-like vibes:

Children Who Chase Lost Voices

"Children who chase lost voices" is a hymn to grief and loss. The movie takes us through a journey between different worlds, showing the dark and the ugly that can occour while experiencing a loss. It's a story about keeping on living while remembering the loved ones.

Violet Evergarden

If I have to think about a comfort anime to watch over and over again it would be "Violet Evergarden". Violet is a 14 year girl used as a weapon in the army, who finds herself picking up pieces of her broken soul after a terrible accident after which she not only loses both arms, but also an important figure in her life. She will need to learn how to be human again and to live with trauma.

Frieren:Beyond Journey's End

Another amazing anime that follows an ancient elf mage, Frieren, who relives the journey she and her companions did 80 years prior. It's a story of love, death and passing of time.
"Slaying the demon king was one chapter but we will live on. We've stories yet to tell and lives yet to live."
